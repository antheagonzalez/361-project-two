My Cat, Loosy

  • I have a fourteen year old American Short Hair named Loosy, she also goes by "Baby Loo" or "Gato".

Quick Information

  • She is a gray tabby cat with white paws (they look like socks!)
  • Her birthday is July 14, 2004.
  • She is adapted and she was the only girl out of all of her siblings.

Fun Facts

  • Yells at my dad to feed her whenever she is hungry
  • 9/10 times she can be found napping, or as I like to call it, "resting from her rest"
  • My dog, Mr. Roach, is afraid of her.
  • She constantly steals my mother's seat at the dining table.
  • She has an Instragram account (@loosykat), it is run by my younger sister.
