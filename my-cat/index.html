My Cat, Loosy

I have a fourteen year old American Short Hair named Loosy, she also goes by "Baby Loo" or "Gato".

Quick Information

  1. She is a gray tabby cat with white paws (they look like socks!)
  2. Her birthday is July 14, 2004.
  3. She is adapted and she was the only girl out of all of her siblings.

Fun Facts

  1. Yells at my dad to feed her whenever she is hungry
  2. 9/10 times she can be found napping, or as I like to call it, "resting from her rest"
  3. My dog, Mr. Roach, is afraid of her.
  4. She constantly steals my mother's seat at the dining table.
  5. She has an Instragram account (@loosykat), it is run by my younger sister.
